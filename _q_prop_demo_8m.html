Simulate the fast reorientation system and demonstrate quaternion propagation Published Demo More...

Detailed Description

The routines are: QProp, QIToBDot, and the JPL propagation from the Wong reference.

References: Wong, E.C. and W.G. Breckenridge, "Inertial Attitude Determination for a Dual-Spin Planetary Spacecraft", AIAA Journal of Guidance

Navigation, and Control Vol. 6, No. 6, Nov-Dec 1983, pp. 491-8.
