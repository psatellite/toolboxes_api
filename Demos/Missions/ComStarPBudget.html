Demonstrate a pointing budget for a typical satellite.

A pointing budget must account for all misalignments and attitude errors. Creates two files, NormalBudget.txt and StationkeepingBudget.txt ------------------------------------------------------------------------- See also PBudget. -------------------------------------------------------------------------
