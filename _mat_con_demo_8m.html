Matrix converter demonstration Published Demo More...

Detailed Description

This is a demo of a dynamical model of the Matrix Converter presented in the reference below. A matrix converter converts n phases to m phases without a DC link. This simplifies the converter design.

See also
delta, ABC2SRF, MC33Switching, SRF2ABC

Reference Nikkhajoei et al "Dynamic Model of a Matrix Converter for Controller Design and System Studies", IEEE Transactions on Power Delivery, vol. 21, no. 2, April 2006, pp 744-754.
