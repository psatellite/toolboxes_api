End-to-end TSTO launch simulation. More...

Detailed Description

Published Demo

This simulates a two stage to orbit vehicle (TSTO). The first stage has a turbofan engine and a ramjet. The second stage is a LO2/LH2 engine. The second stage reaches 380 km goes into a circular orbit and then reenters. Each vehicle has time-varying mass. The propulsion functions are external. The second stage is flown back to the ground.
