Compare the magnetic field models. More...

Detailed Description

Published Demo

Compares the dipole and IGRF models in a polar orbit. Also computes the variations from the dipole of the Mead-Fairfield model.

Things to try: look at other orbits, such as an equatorial orbit, for comparison. Also try different numbers of coefficients in the IGRF model.
