Orbit Module

Orbit mechanics and maneuver planning in Earth orbit. More...

Description

The Orbit module contains tools for orbit mechanics, maneuver planning, propagation and simulation, and trajectory optimization. This includes a variety of gravity models, from point mass to 150 term spherical harmonic models. There is a set of functions specifically for low energy maneuvers in cislunar space. Interplanetary functions including Lambert rendezvous are grouped together.

Browse the Orbit API. View the Orbit Published Demos.
