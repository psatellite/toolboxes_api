Sail Module » Sail Demos

Demonstrate the solar sail disturbance model in heliocentric orbit. More...

Detailed Description

Published Demo

The orbit has an apogee of 1 AU and a perigee of 0.25 AU. Uses the SailWithBoom CAD model. The sail is sun-pointing. The epoch is JD 2000.

Functions demonstrated: QSunPointing SailDisturbance

Since version 7.
