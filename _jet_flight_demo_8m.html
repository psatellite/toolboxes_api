Aircraft takeoff demo. More...

Detailed Description

Published Demo

The aircraft starts on the runway in takeoff mode. When it is moving at the takeoff speed it climbs. You can also try banked turns.

The parameters are for the F-35 fighter. They are only approximate. This demo uses ACPointMassControl.

Things to try:

  1. Switch the mode to turn
  2. Determine the ceiling for the aircraft
  3. Determine the maximum speed for the aircraft

References: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pratt_%26_Whitney_F135 http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/F-35_Lightning_II
