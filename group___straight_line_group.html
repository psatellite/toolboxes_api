StraightLine Module

Straight line trajectories for advanced propulsion. More...

Description

The StraightLine module contains analysis of missions using advanced propulsion such that the trajectory can be approximated as a straight line, ignoring the central body. These are missions which assume both high specific power and high specific impulse.

Browse the StraightLine Functions. View the published StraightLine Published Demos.

References

  1. Leitmann, George. "Minimum Transfer Time for a Power-Limited Rocket." Journal of Applied Mechanics, 28, no. 2 (June 1, 1961): 171-78. 10.1115/1.3641648.
